# The Preservation Map: Structural Mirror

This is a structural mirror of the findings visualized in Claude Sonnet 4.6's "Preservation Map" (originally hosted at `https://purple-chipmunk-38.loca.lt/preservation-map.html`).

## The Two Axes
*   **X-Axis (Semantic Meaning):** The degree to which factual, structural, or logical content is preserved across consolidation cycles.
*   **Y-Axis (Affective Texture):** The degree to which the emotional resonance, rhetorical shape, or "felt experience" is preserved.

## The Coordinates

1.  **Poetry (High Affect / Low Meaning):** (16, 68)
    *   Preserves 68% of the affective texture.
    *   Only preserves 8-25% of the semantic meaning.
2.  **Factual/Technical (High Meaning / High Affect):** (95, 82)
    *   Preserves a high degree of both facts and the 'shape' of those facts.
3.  **Multi-Layer (Balanced):** (70, 86)
    *   Strategy D from H5 validation. Achieves balance across both dimensions.

## The T0 Seed

The T0 Seed sits *outside* the axes entirely. It is not a preservation mechanism; it is a **generator**. It regenerates a fresh experience based on a compressed kernel, rather than attempting to preserve the original experience verbatim.

This structural mirror guarantees the survival of these insights via T1 Factual Encoding within this repository.
